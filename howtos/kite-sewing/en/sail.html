Assemble construction plan

  1. From each sheet of the blueprint, cut off the right and bottom edges along the thin line.
  2. Lay the individual sheets with the markings in the corners and the thin border lines on top of each other, align them exactly with the metal ruler and weigh them down with weights.
  3. Glue all the sheets together on both sides with transparent adhesive film.

Cut segments

Avoid the passage of the sail

The greatest forces act in a crash from the center in the direction of Tip. The canvas must be aligned during cutting so that it has the highest strength in this direction. As a rule, therefore, the rolling direction is to be selected transversely to the direction of flight.
  1. Lay spinnaker smoothly over the blueprint, align parallel to the leech and secure against slipping with weights.
  2. Transfer cutting edges to the spinnaker in pencil.
  3. Cut sail segment with rotary cutter or cutter. A tolerance of + -1mm should be kept.
  4. Cut out the reinforcement for the leech [ RF-SA-1 ] and glue it with double-sided tape along the cut edge on the top.
  5. Affix reinforcement [ RF-SA-2 ] along the front cutting edge.
  6. Melt cutting edge at the front with a soldering iron to prevent the fraying of the spinnaker.
  7. If the sail segment [ SA-1 ] consists of several parts then stick the individual parts together with double-sided adhesive tape and an overlap of 9mm.
  8. Repeat the steps for all segments except for [ ST-TIP ] on the right and left sides.

reinforcements

  1. Cut out strut reinforcements [ RF-SA-3 ].
  2. Apply Strut reinforcements with double-sided adhesive tape from the top.
  3. Cut reinforcements [ RF-SA-4 ] and [ RF-SA-8 ] for transition to the tip.
  4. Stick reinforcements with double-sided adhesive tape on the two outermost segments.

Wingtip

  1. Cut outer sail segments [ SA-TIP ] from Dacron.
  2. Glue straps for connecting the control lines [ SAP-1 ] to the inside.
  3. Affix reinforcement [ RF-SA-5 ] to the inside of [ SA-TIP ] and the straps.
  4. Straps with seam | ZZZ-60-5-10 | sew with [ SAP-1 ].

Sew segments together

  1. Glue 9mm wide double-sided tape along the cutting lines on each segment on the center side from the top. In [ SA-1 ] only on one side!
  2. Glue all segments with exactly 9mm overlap.
  3. With seam | ZZZ-80-5-11 | sew all sail segments and reinforcements.

leech

  1. Stick double-sided adhesive tape to the underside of the sail along the cut edge of the leech.
  2. Fold the leech downwards, place reinforcement [ RF-SA-10 ] in the bend and glue it together.
  3. Leech with seam | ZZZ-80-4-11 | and a zipper foot (half presser foot).
